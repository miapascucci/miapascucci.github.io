Introducing Me!

My name is Mia Pascucci, and I am currently studying at Western Sydney University as I work towards building a future career in publishing and/or journalism. Through both my academic journey and personal interests, I hope to continue developing as a communicator and eventually contribute to the publishing or journalism industry by sharing stories and ideas that connect with others.
